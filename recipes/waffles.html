Waffles

Description:

A waffle is a leavened batter or dough cooked between two hot plates of a waffle iron, patterned to give a characteristic size, shape, and grid-like surface impression. Waffles are cooked until they become golden-brown in color, with a crispy outer texture and a soft interior.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Vanlla
  • Cooking spray

Steps:

  1. Make the batter: Whisk the eggs, then add the flour, milk, and oil. Whisk in the sugar. Stir in the remaining ingredients
  2. Make the waffles: Pour the batter onto the hot, prepared waffle iron. Cook until golden brown and repeat with the remaining batter.

Waffle toppings

  • Blueberry sauce
  • Nuts(such as pecans or walnuts)
  • Vanilla glaze
  • Nutella
  • Homemade whipped cream
  • Fresh berries (such as blueberries or strawberries)
